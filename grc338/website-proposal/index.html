Introduction

Duffy's Poppin' Pizza

Duffy's Poppin' Pizza is pizza chain dedicated to making only gourmet pizzas with a variety of fresh toppings and doughs.

Target audience

People who love pizza! No matter your taste or diet, we have a selction of pizzas for everyone.

Our goal is to give a quality pizza experience to those who look for food outside their comfort zones, or those who want comfort in their food. We not not only serve just pizza, but jumbo shrimp, Poppin' Wings, and slabs of ribs as well! Our mission is to give customers a satisfactory casual dining experience at a low cost.
