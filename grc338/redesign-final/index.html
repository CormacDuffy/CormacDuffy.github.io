Bonestell Studios

Fine Art and Fine Craft

Los Osos, California - 805.528.4265

We are a husband and wife team, each primarily doing our own work and occasionally attempting the glorious collaboration. Please enjoy our site and contact us with any questions or comments you may have. We love to hear from you!

Our work can be seen primarily during the Open Studios Art Tour (San Luis Obispo County, CA) every October and our Holiday Open Studio the first weekend in December. Portions of Jay's collection can also be seen at the annual Morro Bay Winter Bird Festival.
